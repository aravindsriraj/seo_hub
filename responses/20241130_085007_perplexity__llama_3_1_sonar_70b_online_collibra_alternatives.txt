If you are considering alternatives to Collibra for your data governance, intelligence, and management needs, here are some notable options:

## Commercial Alternatives

### Atlan
Atlan is often highlighted for its ease of setup, deep integrations with modern data stack tools like Snowflake, Tableau, and Fivetran, and its intuitive data lineage features. It is known for its DIY setup that can be completed in weeks, unlike the lengthy implementation cycles of legacy platforms like Collibra[2][3].

### Alation
Alation provides an enterprise data intelligence platform that combines machine learning with human insight. It is known for its collaboration capabilities and open interfaces, making it suitable for sectors such as finance, healthcare, and technology[5].

### Semarchy xDM
Semarchy xDM offers a unified data platform that enables discovery, governance, enrichment, and management of data. It is praised for its business-centric interfaces and rapid creation and adoption of data-rich applications[3].

### Zaloni Arena
Zaloni Arena is an end-to-end DataOps platform that focuses on data management, enrichment, and security. It allows for self-service data enrichment and provides detailed visualizations and masking for superior security[3].

### BigID
BigID specializes in data security, privacy, compliance, and governance. It offers a unified platform for data visibility and control, making it a strong competitor in the data governance space[5].

### Qlik Sense
Qlik Sense is a business intelligence and visual analytics platform that supports self-service analytics, interactive dashboards, and custom analytics. It is noted for its ease of use and integration capabilities[4].

### Talend
Talend focuses on data integration, quality, and governance. Its platform is designed to manage and integrate data across various environments, ensuring data quality and compliance[5].

### Immuta
Immuta provides a data security platform with services such as sensitive data discovery and policy management. It is particularly useful for ensuring data security in complex data environments[5].

### Microsoft Purview
Microsoft Purview is a data governance solution that helps organizations manage and govern their data across multiple sources. It integrates well with other Microsoft tools and offers robust data lineage and security features[3].

## Open Source Alternatives

For those looking at open-source options, here are a few alternatives:

### Amundsen
Amundsen is an open-source data discovery and metadata engine that helps in managing and discovering data assets within an organization[2].

### DataHub
DataHub is a metadata platform for data discovery, governance, and observability. It supports various data sources and provides a unified view of metadata[2].

### Apache Atlas
Apache Atlas is a metadata management and governance platform that enables data discovery, data lineage, and data governance. It integrates well with other Apache tools like Hadoop and Spark[2].

### Metacat
Metacat is an open-source metadata catalog that helps in managing and discovering data assets. It is particularly useful for scientific and research communities[2].

### OpenMetadata
OpenMetadata is an open-source metadata platform that provides data discovery, data lineage, and data governance features. It supports various data sources and offers a customizable interface[2].

Each of these alternatives has its unique strengths and may be more suitable depending on your specific needs, such as ease of implementation, integration capabilities, and the depth of data governance features.
