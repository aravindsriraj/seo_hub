### Understanding Data Mesh

Data mesh is a decentralized approach to data architecture and data product management. It emphasizes:

1. **Domain-Oriented Ownership**: Each data domain is owned by the teams that are closest to the data, promoting accountability and expertise.
  
2. **Data as a Product**: Treating data as a product, emphasizing the needs of data consumers and enabling self-service access.

3. **Self-Service Infrastructure**: Providing tools and services that allow teams to independently manage and deliver their own data products.

4. **Federated Computational Governance**: Establishing standard governance practices while allowing teams the autonomy to operate within their domains.
