Data governance, while crucial for data management and compliance, can vary in pricing based on factors like the scale of your data, the complexity of your data governance needs, and whether you're using managed services or building your own solutions. Here's a rough breakdown of costs:

1. **Managed Services (Software-as-a-Service - SaaS):**
   - **Entry-level plans:** $50 - $200 per user/month. These plans often cover basic data governance features like data cataloging, data quality, and access control.
   - **Enterprise plans:** $2,000 - $10,000+ per month. These plans offer advanced features like data lineage, data impact analysis, data privacy and compliance tools, and dedicated customer success managers.

   Examples:
   - Talend: Starts at $750/month for their Data Catalog.
   - Erwin: Starts at $695/user/month for their Data Governance Center.
   -omatix: Starts at $995/user/month for their Enterprise Data Governance platform.

2. **On-premise or Self-Hosted Solutions:**
   - **Entry-level:** $2,000 - $10,000 (one-time license fee).
   - **Enterprise:** $10,000+ (one-time license fee). These typically require additional costs for setup, customization, and maintenance.

   Examples:
   - Informatica: Starting at $15,000 (one-time license fee) for their Enterprise Data Governance solution.
   - SAP Data Governance Steward: $6,500 (one-time license fee) when bundled with SAP Data Hub.

3. **Consulting and Professional Services:**
   - These can range from $50 - $200 per hour for junior consultants to $150 - $300 per hour for senior data governance experts.
   - Implementation projects can cost between $20,000 - $200,000+ depending on the scope.

4. **Open-Source Solutions:**
   - Some open-source tools like Apache Atlas and Collibra's open-core model have a base version for free, but there may be associated costs, such as for support, training, or premium features.

5. **Training and Certification:**
   - Online courses can cost between $100 - $500 per course.
   - Certified Data Governance Professional (CDGP) Certification through DAMA International: $500 (exam fee only).
