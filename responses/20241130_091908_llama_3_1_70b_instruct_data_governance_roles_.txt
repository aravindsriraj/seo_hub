Here are some common data governance roles and their responsibilities:

1. **Data Governance Officer**: Oversees the overall data governance program, developing policies, procedures, and standards for data management.
2. **Data Owner**: Responsible for a specific dataset or data domain, ensuring its accuracy, quality, and security.
3. **Data Steward**: Oversees the quality and integrity of data across multiple datasets or domains.
4. **Data Custodian**: Responsible for the storage, security, and management of data.
5. **Data Analyst/Reporting Specialist**: Responsible for developing reports and analytics to support data-driven decision-making.
6. **Data Scientist**: Responsible for analyzing complex data sets to identify patterns and trends.
7. **Data Quality Specialist**: Responsible for ensuring the quality and accuracy of data, identifying and resolving data-related issues.
8. **Metadata Specialist**: Responsible for developing and maintaining metadata, which provides context and description of data.
9. **Data Security Specialist**: Responsible for ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.
10. **Compliance Officer**: Responsible for ensuring that data governance practices comply with laws, regulations, and organizational policies.

**Data Governance Team Roles**

1. **Data Governance Committee**: Oversees the data governance program, sets policies and standards, and ensures accountability.
2. **Data Management Team**: Responsible for implementing data governance policies and standards, managing data quality and security.
3. **Data Architecture Team**: Designs and implements data architecture to support data governance and integration.

**Data Governance Responsibility Matrix**

| Role | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |

| Data Owner | Ensures data accuracy and quality |

| Data Quality Specialist | Ensures data quality and accuracy |
| Metadata Specialist | Maintains metadata |
| Data Security Specialist | Ensures data security |
| Compliance Officer | Ensures regulatory compliance |

Note: Roles and responsibilities may vary based on the organization's specific needs and requirements. This is just a general overview of common data governance roles.
